About Us

Workshop No. 5 was founded with superb craftsmanship and sustainable design in mind. Driven to inspire and captivate, we create harmonic spaces that are equal parts artful and functional. Our practice is rooted in natural materials. We actively seek out new ways to improve on old schools of architecture by upcycling and sourcing sustainable materials. Although we draw inspiration from modern schools of design, our work is timeless in nature. Workshop No. 5 approaches each project through collaboration, understanding, and elevated design. We facilitate open and communicative dialogues with our clients that lead to outstanding outcomes. Simply imaginative, our work always incorporates creative solutions that result in beautiful yet functional spaces that transcend expectations.
